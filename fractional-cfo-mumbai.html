What is a Fractional CFO?

A fractional CFO provides full CFO-level financial leadership—working capital structuring, MIS reporting, board presentations, and lender negotiations—on a part-time retainer. Unlike a full-time hire at ₹50L+ annually, you engage for specific outcomes at 20–40% of that cost.
